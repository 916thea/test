About Us

For over 50 years, SEIWA OPTICAL has been a provider of customizable optical solutions for machine vision, inspection, and industrial processing.

Machine Vision
We provide a wide selection of objectives, telecentric lenses, and illumination products.

Microscopes We provide laser ready microscope systems for probe stations and substrate repair, along with a selection of stereo and digital microscopes.

Process Equipment
We provide process equipment for laser repair, inspection, mask aligner, and film exposure systems.
